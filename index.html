Diving into deep learning


Neural networks are in the spotlight of the current artificial intelligence revolution. We are migrating from intelligent solutions built over a set of rules to solutions that extract information over sets of data [1]. The current avaliability of data and computational resources allowed researches to create and train neural networks of several layers, hence the name deep learning.

These networks are able to extract meaningful information from large amounts of data and currently present the best results in image, audio and text processing [2]. These results are at the same level of humans or even better (superhuman) but although the excitement is justified we are still far from skynet and the terminator (working on it).

This website presents a series of explanations over basic concepts related to artificial neural networks. The main idea is to present the concepts and simple implementation code in python to clarify how neural network really works.
