Diving into deep learning


The current developments in Artificial Intelligence have the potential to transform our society. Several researches recently published could endow the computer with superhuman habilities. Since these researches are still in early stages much more advancements are expected in a near future.

AI is migrating from solutions built over sets of rules (symbolic AI) to solutions that extract information over sets of data (distributed representations) [1]. The current availability of data and computational resources allowed researches to create and train machine learning algorithms of several abstraction levels, hence the name deep learning. These models are able to extract meaningful information from large amounts of data and currently present the best results in image, audio and text processing [2]. These results are at the same level of humans or even better (superhuman) but although the excitement is justified we are still far from skynet and the terminator (working on it, sorry Elon Musk).

This website presents a series of explanations over basic concepts related to deep learning. The main idea is to present the concepts and simple implementation code in python to clarify how AI solutions really works.
